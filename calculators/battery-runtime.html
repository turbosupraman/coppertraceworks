Equation

Reference

t = (C_mAh × η) / I_mA

η = usable fraction. Lithium cells typically derate to 80%. Lead-acid to 50%.

Usage note

Practical
Real runtimes are shorter than ideal due to Peukert's effect (discharge rate), temperature, age, and cutoff voltage. Use 80% usable capacity for Li-ion. For lead-acid at high discharge rates, 50% is more realistic.
