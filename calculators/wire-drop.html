Equations

Reference

R_wire = 2 × ρ × L / A

V_drop = I × R_wire

P_loss = I² × R_wire

Factor of 2 accounts for round trip (both conductors).

Usage note

Practical
Length entered is one-way; the calculator doubles it for the return conductor. Resistance values are for solid wire at 20 °C. Stranded wire of the same AWG has slightly higher resistance. Ampacity (safe current) limits are not checked here — consult NEC tables for current ratings.
